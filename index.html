Neha Tyagi

509 E White st - 21,

I am a theoretical chemist currently working as a post-doctoral research associate in UIUC. In my Ph.D., I have built on several mathematical models to understand various stochastic process in condensed phase systems. In my current role, I am more concerned about dynamics and energetics of ubiquitous polymeric systems under flows. The combination of mathematical tools and computational techniques can be well employed to understand the behavior of polymeric and soft materials. This knowledge can be applied to the designing of high performance materials.
